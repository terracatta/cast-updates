Cast

Native macOS lighting control for the HyperX QuadCast 2 S microphone.

Download Cast 1.0
macOS 14 (Sonoma) or newer · Universal binary · Notarized

What it does

  • 15 lighting effects across Colors, Patterns, Scenes, and Audio-reactive categories
  • Time-of-day color — lighting drifts with the actual time
  • Audio-reactive modes (EQ, Prism, VU, Waterfall) react to your mic input in real time
  • Lives in the menubar, no Dock icon
  • Hot-plug aware — connects automatically when you plug in

Privacy

Cast doesn't collect or transmit any data. The microphone permission, when used by audio-reactive modes, processes audio locally to compute frequency bands — nothing is recorded, stored, or sent anywhere.
